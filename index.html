About

Hey folks! I'm Reinan, but you can just call me "Laki" or "Lucky" – that's what my pals call me! Recently graduated from ACLC College of Daet with a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology, and let me tell you, I'm brimming with excitement for the road ahead. Whether I'm diving into coding or exploring graphic design, I'm all about immersing myself in the tech world with a big grin on my face!


My time at ACLC was a whirlwind of fun – from pulling all-nighters with my amazing classmates to conquering those tricky projects together. But now, it's time to take that energy and bring it out into the real world. Tech is my jam, and I'm psyched to soak up wisdom from the pros and maybe even blaze my own trail.


When I'm not glued to my screen, you'll catch me gaming, jamming to tunes, fueling up in the kitchen or cooking. So, here's to new adventures and endless possibilities. Let's kick off this tech journey with a high-five and a smile!
